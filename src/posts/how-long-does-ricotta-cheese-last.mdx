Ricotta cheese is a soft, white cheese that is made from whey, a by-product of the production of mozzarella and other cheeses. Ricotta cheese has a mild flavor and a slightly grainy texture. It is often used in lasagna, cannelloni, and ravioli dishes. How long does ricotta cheese last? To find an answer, please read on.

## How long does ricotta cheese last?

![How long does ricotta cheese last](/img/how-long-does-ricotta-cheese-last-300x200.jpg)



| **Ricotta Cheese** | **Last in Fridge**          |
| ------------------ | --------------------------- |
| Unopened           | “Best-By” date + 3 – 5 days |
| Opened             | 1-2 weeks                   |

Ricotta cheese will last for about 1-2 weeks in the fridge once opened. After that, it will start to spoil and should be thrown out. Ricotta cheese can also be frozen for up to 3 months. When thawing, make sure to do so in the fridge and not at room temperature. Once thawed, cheese should be used within 2-3 days.

## How to tell if ricotta cheese is bad?

Ricotta cheese that has gone bad will have a sour smell and may also have mold growing on it. The texture of the cheese will also be different; it will be dry and crumbly. If you see any of these signs, throw the cheese out.

## How to store ricotta cheese properly?

The best way to store cheese is in the fridge in an airtight container. Make sure to use it within 1-2 weeks of opening. If you plan on freezing the ricotta cheese, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and then place it in a freezer-safe bag. When thawing, be sure to do so in the fridge.

## Can you freeze an unopened container of ricotta cheese?

Yes, cheese can last in the fridge for a couple of weeks or in the freezer for about two months. However, if you freeze ricotta cheese, it’s best to thaw it before using it. Ricotta cheese can also be stored in the **[pantry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pantry)** for a few days. However, if it starts to smell sour or rancid, it’s best to throw it out. Ricotta cheese is a versatile cheese that can be used in sweet or savory dishes. It’s also a great addition to smoothies and baking recipes.

## What does bad ricotta smell like?

Cheese can start to smell sour if it goes bad. The cheese will also start to change color and get a slimy texture. There will also be mold growing on the surface of the cheese that has gone bad. If any of these signs are present, it is best to discard the cheese.

## Why is my ricotta cheese watery?

Ricotta cheese is a fresh cheese and has high water content. If it has been stored for too long, the water will start to separate from the rest of the cheese. This is perfectly normal and does not mean that the cheese has gone bad. You can just stir it back together before using it.

## Final Thoughts

Ricotta cheese is a versatile cheese that can be used in both sweet and savory dishes. It has a mild flavor and a slightly grainy texture. Ricotta cheese will last for 1-2 weeks in the fridge once opened. Be sure to store it in an airtight container and use it within 2-3 days of opening.
