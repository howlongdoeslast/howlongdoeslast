## What causes Gonorrhea?

Gonorrhea is caused by a bacterium called Neisseria gonorrhoeae. It can be passed through vaginal, anal, or oral sex. It can also be passed from an infected mother to her baby during childbirth.

## How is Gonorrhea treated?
