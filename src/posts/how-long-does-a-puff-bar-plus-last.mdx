To make your Puff bar works more efficiently and last longer, you should follow these tips:

* Keep the puff bar clean by washing it regularly with warm water and drying it with a paper towel.
* Use the puff bar right away after you finish charging it to get more battery life.
* You should wait for a few seconds between puffs so you can let the heating coil cool down, which will make your puff bar last longer.
* Never overfill the cartridge with e-liquid because that might cause leakage and damage your puff bar.\
