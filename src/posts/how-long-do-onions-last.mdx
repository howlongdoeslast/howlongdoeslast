Do you have bought so many onions that you don’t know what to do with them? Onions are a great vegetable to have on hand because they last for quite some time. However, there are a few things you need to do in order to store them properly and ensure they last as long as possible.

The first thing you need to do is **how long do onions last** so you know how to store them. Onions usually last for around two to four weeks in the fridge, but they can also be frozen. If you want to freeze them, peel and chop them into small pieces before freezing. However, you can store uncut raw onions for 2-3 months at 45-55° F temperature.

## How long do onions last in the fridge?

* **Fresh whole onions: Last 2 – 3 months**
* **Sliced or chopped onions: Last 1 week**

  Onions can last for two weeks in the fridge. So if you are going to store your raw onions then the best way is to keep them in a plastic bag and put them in the fridge. Make sure to keep them away from other fruits and vegetables because they will start to rot.

  ## How long do onions last at room temperature?

  Onions can last for four weeks at room temperature. If you are going to store your onions this way, make sure to keep them in a cool, dark place. You can also put them in a mesh bag and hang them in the fridge.

  ## How long do onions last on the counter?

  You can store whole onions on the counter for six weeks. If you want to keep it for longer then refrigerate them.

  ## Can I freeze onions?

  Yes, you can freeze onions. In fact, freezing is the best way to store them for a long time. To freeze them, peel and chop them into small pieces before freezing. Frozen onions will last for around six to eight months.

  ## How to store onions properly?

  Here are some tips that you can follow to preserve onions for months.

  ### Store in a cool and dry place

  If you are going to store your onions at room temperature then find a place like a **[pantry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pantry)** where it is cool and dry. The onions will not last as long as if they were in the fridge but they will still last around 3-4 weeks.

  ### Store in a mesh bag

  If you have a lot of onions or you want to buy them in bulk then you can store them in a mesh bag. This also allows air to circulate so the onions will not rot. You can also hang the mesh bag in your refrigerator.

  ### Store in fridge

  The best way to store onions is in the fridge. They will last around 2-4 weeks this way. Just make sure to keep them in a cool, dark place and away from other fruits and vegetables.

  ### Freeze them

  If you want to store onions for a longer time then the best way to do so is by freezing them. You can chop them into small pieces before freezing or you can leave them whole. They will last for around 6-8 months this way.
