  ## How long does cooked quinoa last in the fridge?


  Seven days is the answer. If you keep it in an air-tight container, it will last a little longer. However, after that time period, the quinoa will start to spoil and develop a bad taste. So if you have any leftovers, be sure to discard them and do not eat them.

  What is quinoa? Quinoa (Chenopodium quinoa) is a crop that is related to spinach and beets. It has been cultivated in the Andes region of South America for over 3,000 years. Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning that it contains all of the essential amino acids our bodies need to build and maintain muscle tissue. It is also high in fiber and low in calories.


  ## How to tell if cooked quinoa is bad?


  The best way to tell if cooked quinoa is bad is to look for signs of spoilage, such as mold, an off odor, or slimy texture. If you see any of these signs, discard the cooked quinoa. Cooked quinoa will typically last for 7 days in the fridge. However, it’s a good idea to eat it sooner rather than later to avoid any potential health risks.   ## How to store quinoa after cooking?


  Once you’ve cooked the quinoa, it’s important to store it in a sealed container in the fridge. This will help keep it fresh and prevent any potential contamination. Cooked quinoa can last for up to 7 days in the fridge.


  ## Can I freeze cooked quinoa?


  You can freeze cooked quinoa, but it’s important to do so properly to avoid any potential food safety issues. First, make sure to cool the quinoa completely before freezing it. Then, transfer it to a freezer-safe container and store it in the freezer for up to 3 months. Next, thaw the quinoa in the fridge overnight and reheat it before eating when you’re ready to eat it.


  ## How long does cooked quinoa last at room temperature?


  The short answer is two hours. However, it’s best to eat cooked quinoa within an hour of cooking it to avoid any potential food safety issues. If you need to store it for longer than that, be sure to keep it in a sealed container in the fridge.

  ## Can quinoa be eaten raw?


  Yes, quinoa can be eaten raw. However, it’s important to make sure that it is properly cooked before consuming it. Raw quinoa can contain harmful bacteria that can cause food poisoning. Cooked quinoa is safe to eat and provides more nutrients and health benefits than raw quinoa.
