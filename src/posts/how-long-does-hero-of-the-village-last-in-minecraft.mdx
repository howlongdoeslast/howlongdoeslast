## How Long Does Hero of the Village Last?

Hero of the Village lasts for ~2. It is the best feature to give you trades and discounts at the village’s shops.

* First, you need to create a new world (in creative mode)
* Before killing the first zombie, break all the leaves in your area
* Then proceed to kill zombies which are very close to any village for 1 minute. If you don’t get the Hero of the Village title after that time, then leave the game and rejoin
* After rejoining, kill zombies in the village.
* If you don’t get the Hero of the Village title, then go to step 1 and try again (you can leave the game after that)
* After this process, you should be getting your twice in 2-4 days (every second day). If not, press the “Do It Again” button to repeat the process.
* When you get the Hero of the Village title, stop killing zombies in villages for some time (leave the game)
* Then leave the game and start this procedure again after joining a new world.
